Skribbl.db

About

This is a website that helps you keep track of your custom word sets for the classic online game Skribbl.io. I always found that whenever I played that it was more fun to include custom words my friends and I used, but making them was always a hassle because we would forget which words we used last time and we would have to make sure the formatting was right. Then sharing it was also a pain. So I decided I wanted to make a website where it could be stored for us online and we could easily access the custom words we used last time. I hope you enjoy!
